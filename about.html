Executive Leadership

The Leader Behind Defcon

Lefa Mofokeng J.

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

Alexander Sterling founded Defcon in 2010 with a mission to transform the cybersecurity landscape. With a background in cryptography and artificial intelligence, Alexander saw the growing need for proactive security measures in an increasingly digital world.

Prior to Defcon, Alexander led security initiatives at multiple Fortune 500 companies, where he identified critical gaps in enterprise security approaches. This experience inspired him to create a company that would address these vulnerabilities through innovation and expertise.

Under Alexander's leadership, Defcon has pioneered several industry-first technologies, including our patented predictive threat analytics platform and adaptive defense systems.
