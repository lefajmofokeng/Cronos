Security First, Always | Hyperion

Security First, Always

As a leading IT solutions provider focused on data security, compliance, and secure infrastructure, our commitment to our customers is built on trust. We believe that security and data privacy are the foundations of achieving successful digital transformation.

Our Philosophy

Security and Privacy by Design and By Default

We drive a Zero Trust, Defence in Depth security strategy across our systems and platforms. Data privacy assessments are built into our processes to safeguard your personal information.

Empowering a Growth Mindset

To continually strengthen our security posture, we invest heavily in ongoing security and privacy awareness training for all staff.

Hyperion

Setting the highest standard for transparency and accountability

Certifications and Assessments

Hyperion is built on a solid foundation of security, privacy and compliance. We adhere to global standards including ISO/IEC 27001, GDPR, and SOC 2 Type II compliance. We are independently assessed at the highest level for both NIST Cybersecurity and Privacy Frameworks. Hyperion also engages globally recognised security consulting and auditing firms to stress test and audit our core infrastructure systems.

Vulnerability Disclosure Program

Hyperion recognises the importance of security researchers in helping to keep our platform safe. We encourage responsible disclosure of security vulnerabilities via our external program on the leading platform, Hacker One (or similar).
