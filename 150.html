The Enterprise IT Service Delivery Pipeline

1. Requirements Discovery

Conduct thorough business analysis to define scope, objectives, technical needs, and critical success factors.

2. Solution Architecture

Design the optimal cloud, network, and application architecture, ensuring scalability, security, and integration points.

3. Agile Development/Build

Iterative development and configuration using DevOps practices, continuous integration, and rapid prototyping.

4. Test and Deploy

Rigorous testing, security hardening, and phased rollout to production environments following zero-downtime protocols.

5. Monitoring & Optimization

Establish performance baselines and continuous monitoring for proactive issue detection and cost optimization.

6. Ongoing Support

Dedicated, 24/7 managed support and maintenance to ensure service stability and end-user satisfaction.
