Your Path to Launch

Our simplified process ensures your project moves smoothly from idea to deployment with clear milestones.

  1. Initial Consultation: Schedule a 30-minute discovery call to discuss your vision, budget, and timeline.
  2. Define Scope & Quote: We create a detailed Statement of Work (SOW) outlining features, tech stack, and a fixed-price or time & materials estimate.
  3. Contract & Team Onboarding: Sign off on the SOW, and we immediately assign your dedicated design and development team.
  4. Kick-off & First Sprint: The team begins the Discovery Phase, delivering wireframes and the first functional code in under two weeks.
  5. Continuous Delivery: Review weekly or bi-weekly demos, providing feedback until the product is ready for a successful launch.
