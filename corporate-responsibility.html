The Challenge

Technology's Environmental Impact

The digital revolution comes with a significant environmental cost that demands immediate attention and innovative solutions.

Energy Consumption

Data centers consume approximately 1% of global electricity, with projections showing significant growth in the coming years.

E-Waste Generation

Over 50 million tons of electronic waste is generated annually, with less than 20% being properly recycled.

Carbon Emissions

The IT sector accounts for about 2-4% of global carbon emissions, comparable to the aviation industry.

Resource Depletion

Manufacturing electronics requires rare earth minerals and precious metals, contributing to resource scarcity.
